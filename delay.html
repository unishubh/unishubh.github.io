Delaying an SIP?

Are you planning to delay investing? Check how much your delay can cost you?

This calculator helps you understand the impact of delaying your systematic investment by a certain month(s).

You need to input the monthly investment amount, the investment tenure, the expected rate of return and the expected delay in investment. It displays the maturity amount if investment was spread over planned number of years and the maturity amount in the delayed number of months and the cost of delay.
