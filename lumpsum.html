STP(Systematic Transfer Plan) - It is a method of investing in mutual funds. Under the Systematic Transfer Plan (STP) method of investing an investor transfers a fixed amount of money from one category of the fund ( Liquid Funds ) to another in a fixed interval- usually from a debt fund to an equity fund

Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP)- is a facility offered by mutual funds through which an investor can withdraw a pre-determined amount at pre-decided intervals from his/ her investments in select mutual fund schemes.
