What is EMI?

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) – Your equated monthly installment or more commonly known as the EMI is the amount you owe to the lender every month. It includes a part of principal repayment and interest due. ... Compounding Interest is the rate of interest applicable to the loan taken. It is simply the interest due added to the principal amount.

The chief benefit of an EMI is to make your personal budgeting process easier. Mostly Pay to settle their Loans in the Last stage when the Most part of their EMI is the Principal Remained, Its better to settle the EMI in the starting when a major part of EMI Is interest Portion.
